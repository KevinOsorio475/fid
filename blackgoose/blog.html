Low and Slow

This week i am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In this bood, Cooking for Greeks, Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low-temperature poaching.
